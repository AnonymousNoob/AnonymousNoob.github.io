I'm 21 years old and have spent most of my time on computers . Ever since I was a kid I've always wondered what went on behind that 10 inch monitor of mine that I owned for around 12 years . I started using computers when I was 6 years old and I loved gaming . The first ever game that I played was "Dangerous Dave" and to see something move on the screen as I pressed random keys on the keyboard made life much astounding . I've had highs and lows but I've always come back to this one thing - my favourite game .

As I grew up I saw opportunities that would allow me to tell the computer to do as I wanted . That's when the programming started . I spent most of my schooling learning about life as such . Programming to me means just that . It all started off with Photoshop where I would create web pages for random things that went through my mind . I never learnt web Development as such . It all made sense when I saw what Photoshop rendered for me , an index.html file . This exposed me to a whole new space - web Dev . Initially I found UI designing more interesting , then I came across an awesome platform "javascript" . I scripted using js for almost all my works since then .

Then came the era of "python" . As much as I loved C++,Java I started giving no fucks about it after I started exploring python . Python in my point of view is by far the best language suitable for lazy programmers like me (never declared a variable ever since!) . And its so secure and faster and has a lot of support . I've used python mostly for Scrapping and of course "Django". Undoubtedly Django is my favourite framework out there for web Development , it's niche . All that being said , I currently work at an awesome "Startup" where both my Development and Entrepreneurial skills are put to test .
